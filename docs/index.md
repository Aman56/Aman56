AI/ML Scientist / Foundation Models (Personalization) / LLM Interpretability

Welcome to my digital space. I am currently building at **Resonate Networks**, where I lead the development of natural language audience-building engines. 

Previously, I completed my Master's in Computer Science at NYU, researching under Prof. Gus Xia. With five years of experience architecting foundation models for production and scaling complex systems on AWS, my work has been published at top-tier conferences including **NeurIPS** and **ICML**. 

My research interests are constantly evolving. Right now, I'm focused on modeling user behavior to enhance personalization through synthetic respondents and behavior simulation. Past explorations include music-AI, information retrieval, language theory, Responsible AI, and mechanistic interpretability.
